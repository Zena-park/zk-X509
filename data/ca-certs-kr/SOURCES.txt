Korea CA Certificates - Download Sources
=========================================

These certificates are from the Korean NPKI (National Public Key Infrastructure).

Root CAs are distributed via KISA (Korea Internet & Security Agency).
Intermediate CAs are issued by licensed Korean CAs (공인인증기관).

Source: Korean NPKI trust store / individual CA repositories
- KISA: https://www.rootca.or.kr/
- SignKorea (한국전자인증): https://www.signkorea.com/
- CrossCert (한국전자인증): https://www.crosscert.com/
- TradeSign (한국무역정보통신): https://www.tradesign.net/
- yessign (금융결제원): https://www.yessign.com/
