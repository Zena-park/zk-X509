Japan CA Certificates - Download Sources
=========================================

1. GPKI - Bridge CA (政府認証基盤 ブリッジ認証局)
   Source: https://www.gpki.go.jp/selfcert/self_cert.html
   - GPKI-Bridge-CA.der      — BridgeCA self-signed (expires 2034)
     URL: https://www.gpki.go.jp/bca/BCAself.der

2. GPKI - Official Status CA (官職認証局)
   Source: https://www.gpki.go.jp/selfcert/self_cert.html
   - GPKI-OSCA-Root.der      — OfficialStatusCA root (expires 2034)
     URL: https://www.gpki.go.jp/osca/OSCAroot.der

3. JGCA - Japan Government CA (日本政府認証局, WebTrust certified)
   Source: https://www.gpki.go.jp/selfcert/jgca_cert.html
   - JGCA-Root.der           — Japanese Government Root CA (expires 2048)
     URL: https://www.gpki.go.jp/jgca/jgrootca.cer
   - JGCA-OS-SubCA.der       — OfficialStatus Sub CA (expires 2038)
     URL: https://www.gpki.go.jp/jgca/ossubca.cer
   - JGCA-Org-SubCA.der      — Organization Sub CA (expires 2039)
     URL: https://www.gpki.go.jp/jgca/osubca.cer

4. JPKI - My Number Card CAs (公的個人認証サービス)
   Source (Sign): https://www.jpki.go.jp/ca/ca_rules3.html
   Source (Auth): https://www.jpki.go.jp/ca/ca_rules4.html
   - JPKI-Sign-CA-03.der     — Signature CA (署名用, expires 2033)
     URL: https://www.jpki.go.jp/ca/pdf/signca03.cer
   - JPKI-Auth-CA-03.der     — Authentication CA (利用者証明用, expires 2033)
     URL: https://www.jpki.go.jp/ca/pdf/authca03.cer

Fingerprint verification:
- GPKI/JGCA: https://www.gpki.go.jp/selfcert/finger_print.html
- JPKI Sign: https://www.jpki.go.jp/ca/pdf/sign_fingerprint.pdf
- JPKI Auth: https://www.jpki.go.jp/ca/pdf/auth_fingerprint.pdf

Note: LGPKI (地方公共団体 PKI) certs at http://www.lgpki.jp/ were not downloadable externally.
